Frequently Asked Questions

Expert answers to common pest control questions

Are your treatments safe for children and pets?

Yes, we prioritize safety by using eco-friendly, low-toxicity solutions that are effective against pests while being safe for families and pets. We'll provide specific safety instructions for each treatment.

How long does a typical treatment take?

Most residential treatments take 1-2 hours, depending on the property size and pest issue. We'll provide a specific time estimate during the initial inspection.

Do you offer any guarantees?

Yes, we provide a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If pests return within the warranty period, we'll return at no additional cost until the problem is resolved.
