How quickly can SN Pest Control respond to a pest emergency in Montreal?

We provide 24/7 emergency pest control service throughout Montreal and can typically respond within 2-4 hours for urgent situations. Our emergency response covers all areas including downtown Montreal, Laval, Longueuil, and surrounding areas. For non-emergency situations, we usually schedule same-day or next-day appointments.

Are your pest control treatments safe for children and pets in Montreal homes?

Yes, safety is our top priority. We use eco-friendly, low-toxicity pest control solutions that are effective against pests while being safe for families and pets. All our treatments meet Health Canada safety standards and Quebec regulations. We provide specific safety instructions for each treatment and can accommodate families with special sensitivity concerns.

We offer free inspections and provide detailed, no-obligation quotes for all services. Emergency services may have additional charges.

Does SN Pest Control offer guarantees on their Montreal pest control services?

Yes, we provide a 100% satisfaction guarantee on all our pest control services in Montreal. If pests return within the warranty period (varies by service), we'll return at no additional cost until the problem is completely resolved. Our warranty covers both the effectiveness of the treatment and our service quality.

We serve all areas of Greater Montreal including Montreal (all boroughs), Laval, Longueuil, West Island, South Shore, and North Shore.
